Designing and Developing a STEM Program for Middle School Students in Underserved Areas

"Our goal is to connect the students' interests to relevant and meaningful learning experiences in the real world." - Clearview Mission Statement

Creating the Clearview STEM program, I focused on integrating student interests, connecting STEM to real-world experiences, and fostering engagement through hands-on activities. The curriculum aimed to be engaging,enjoyable, and technology-driven, introducing students to STEM opportunities. Unique to this program was the emphasis on student choice, allowing them to explore topics of personal interest, enhancing their ownership and investment in learning.

I feel the end product aligns with Clearview’s mission statement, and is a reflection of the school’s values.
